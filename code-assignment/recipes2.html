Shrimp Thai Curry Soup

Ingredients

Shrimp

  • 2 pounds small raw shrimp

Soup Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper flake
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 large shallot minced
  • 3 tablespoon garlic - jared is fine
  • 2 jalapenos deseeded and small dice
  • 1 serrano pepper small dice
  • 1 two inch ginger root minced
  • 6 limes split
  • 3 14oz Cans Coconut Milk
  • 1 1/2 Quarts of Chicken Stock
  • 2 Tbls Sambal Oelek Chili Paste
  • 3 Tbls Curry Powder
  • 2 oz Red Curry Paste

Rice

  • 2 Cups Basmati Rice

Garnishment

  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Chopped Green Onions
  • Lime Wedges

Preperation

Peel and mince the Shallots, deseed the Jalapenos - small dice, small dice the Serrano, peel and mince the Ginger Root. Zest two limes - You will need to Juice about four limes about 2/3 to 1/2 cup.

In a stock pot add two tables spoons of Olive Oil and the Crushed Red Peppers flake. Once the oil is hot Add Shallots saute until soft about five minutes. Add the Garlic, Jalapenos, Serranos and Ginger saute for for five minutes. Add Lime zest.

Recommendation: Rinse the rice in a large bowl four to five times or until the water is clear. This removes the starch for the rice and provides a better consistancy of the cooked rice. Start Cooking the Basmati Rice per-direction - Usally four cups water to two cup rice.

Add Coconut Milk and Chicken Stock and bring to Boil. Reduce heat to low and add Curry Power, Red Curry Paste, and the Sambal Oelek mix well and let simmer about 10 minutes.

Add Shrimp about five to seven minutes before serving - Do Not over cook the shrimp -

In a bowl serve 1/2 cup rice and laddle the Thai Curry Soup over the rice. Serve with the garnishment: Cilantro, Green Onions, and Lime Wedge.
