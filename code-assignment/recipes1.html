Lemon Cream Pasta

Home Recipes Chris's Place Contact Page

A family favorite and most requested meal

Lemon Cream Pasta Dinner with family!

Ingredients

Shrimp

  • 2 lbs Large Raw Shrimp
  • 4 oz Butter
  • 2 Tbls Garlic - Minced
  • 1 tsp Crushed Red Peper Flake

Lemon Cream Sauce

  • 1 Tbls Crushed Red Pepper Flake
  • 4 oz Butter
  • 1 lrg Shallot ~1/2 Cup
  • 2 Tbls Lemon Zest
  • 3/4 Cup Lemon Jusice - Fresh
  • 1 Pint Heavy Cream
  • 12 oz Fresh Spinach - Optional
  • 16 oz Fresh Fettuccine Noodles
  • Chopped Italian Parsley for garnishment

Directions

In a large pasta pot bring water to boil with salt add to the water.

Peel and mince Shallots. Zest two lemons and retain the juice of about four lemons about 3/4 to 1 cup of juice.

In a sauce pan melt the butter and crushed red peppers flake. Add Shallots saute until soft about five minutes. Add Lemon Zest and saute for about two minutes. Add Heavy Cream bring to simmer about five minutes. Add Lemon Juice to thicken sauce. Simmer on low.

Add Pasta to boiling water and cook fresh pasta for five to seven minutes. Drain and move past to a pasta bowl. Add Spinach and blanch two to three minutes.

In a large Skillet melt butter add Crushed Red Pepper Flake, and Garlic. Add Shrimp in a single layer, you may need to do the shrimp in batches. Cook between five and eight minutes. DO NOT Over cook the Shrimp.

Add the blanched Spinach to the pasta bowl and toss togther. Now add the Lemon Cream Sauce and mix well. Serve with Shrimp! Enjoy! Bon Appetit.
